Home/Story

We make fewer things, and we make them well.

OBJET began in a small Montréal workshop with one stubborn rule: design nothing that can't be repaired. Everything we release is prototyped by hand, lived with for years, and built from materials we can stand behind long after the sale.

The studio floor · Montréal, 2025
Repairable

Built to come apart

Every joint unscrews, every part is sold on its own. A scratch or a worn cable should never mean the whole thing is finished.

Honest

Materials, as they are

Solid wood, brass, glass and natural fibre — left to age and patina rather than coated to fake permanence. What you see is the material.

Patient

Made in small batches

We release a handful of objects a year. Each one earns its place by being better than what it replaces, or it doesn't ship.

(How we got here)

A short history.

2019

One bench, one rule

The studio opened with a single product and a promise to repair it for life. It sold out, and the repair queue stayed empty.

2021

The lighting collection

We learned to blow glass with a local maker and released the Halo Pendant — our first object designed to be taken apart in under a minute.

2023

Carbon-neutral delivery

Every order now ships carbon-neutral in recyclable packaging, with a take-back programme for anything that reaches the end of its life.

2025

Forty-one objects

Three rooms — lighting, seating, tabletop — and a ten-year guarantee on all of it. Still small, still slow, still by hand.
